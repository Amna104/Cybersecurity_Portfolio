## **The 5 W’s**

### **Who**
An organized group of unethical hackers.

### **What**
A ransomware security incident that encrypted critical systems and data.

### **Where**
At a healthcare company.

### **When**
Tuesday, 9:00 a.m.

### **Why**
Attackers used a phishing email to gain unauthorized access to internal systems.  
After gaining entry, they deployed ransomware and encrypted critical health records.  
Their motivation appears to be financial, based on a ransom demand for the decryption key.

---

## **Additional Notes**
**Questions to consider:**
- How can the healthcare company prevent an incident like this in the future?  
  - Implement phishing awareness training  
  - Strengthen email filtering  
  - Enforce MFA  
  - Maintain offline, encrypted backups  
  - Improve incident response readiness  

- Should the company pay the ransom?  
  - Paying the ransom does *not* guarantee data recovery  
  - It may encourage further attacks  
  - The better course is to rely on secure backups and IR policies
